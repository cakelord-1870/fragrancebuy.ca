LALIQUE

Lalique is a historic French glassmaking house which was founded in 1888. Despite being primarily known for their opulent, sculptural creations within the glassware industry, Lalique have also crafted equally exquisite olfactory offerings for the designer perfumery market. Working with legendary noses such as Benoist Lapouza and Nathalie Lorson, Lalique have developed a repertoire of nearly one hundred fine fragrances for both men and women to date. On our website, we regularly carry fan favorite offerings from the Encre Noire Line, as well as exclusive releases from both the Noir Premier and Compositions Parfumees lines, all at the lowest prices available online.
